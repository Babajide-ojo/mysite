About Me

Babajide Ojo holds a second class upper division in Pure and Applied Mathematics from the Federal University of Agriculture, Abeokuta, Ogun State, his areas of interest span through software development, teaching, and budgeting.

Babajide is the Assistant Headteacher in Ansach school and doubles as a web developer, with over two years of experience. During his industrial attachment, he served as a team lead of a group of trainees. He was an active member of the planning committee in preparing the proposed budget for 2017 in a local government area in Lagos. He runs a personal outreach to teenagers on sex education and serves as a career advisor to children. Babajide is vision-oriented with a high sense of diligence, and he is committed to delivering excellence.
